About Hayagreeva

Who are we?

Hayagreeva as a company (started in 2007) has always focused on Web Content Management and Digital Experience Management. Hayagreeva started off in Mumbai, India as services partner for Interwoven (then maker of TeamSite and alike CMS products). The company has since then focused on this domain only with a major focus on Banks and Fintech clients.

After successful operations in India, the company has created footprint globally. Hayagreeva has so far enabled clients like Standard Chartered, DBS, OCBC, ICICI Bank, HDFC Bank, SBI Cards, etc. to not only improve its digital experience but also to take care of its compliance needs with regards to content sanity and accountability.

With a total experience of 400+ man-years on the various Web Technologies, Hayagreeva is possibly the largest practice in Asia having presence in multiple locations across India and Singapore.
