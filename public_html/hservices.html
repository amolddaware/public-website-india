About Qatar

The State of Qatar is one of the world's most important producers of oil and gas.

HH the Amir

The Amir is the Head of State. His person shall be inviolable and he must be respected by all.

Qatar National Vision 2030

The National Vision 2030 is a master vision and roadmap towards Qatar becoming an advanced society.

Government & Legislatives

Qatar’s government structure includes ministries, supreme councils, and other government agencies.

The Constitution

The constitutions states that defending the nation is the responsibility of every citizen.

Shura Council

The Shura council was established since 1972 to discuss the state affairs, laws, and policies

Central Municipal Council

The Central Municipal Council monitors the execution of laws and regulations of municipal affairs.

National Flag

This flag has a unique story and a long history behind its current form, engraved in our hearts.

National Day

HH Sheikh Jassim bin Mohammed Al Thani was the first founder of modern Qatar on December 18, 1887.

National Sport Day

National Sport day is viewed as an opportunity to bring communities closer together through sport.

Sustainable Development Goals

The State of Qatar is keen on achieving the sustainable development goals and agendas of 2030.
